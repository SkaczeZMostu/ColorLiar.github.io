Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ColorLiar ("we", "us") collects, uses, and discloses information through our mobile game "ColorLiar" (the "Service").

2. Data Collection

2.1 Unity Analytics and Unity Ads

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Unity Analytics: Collects anonymous gameplay data (e.g., session length, level progress) to improve the game.
  • Unity Ads: Displays personalized ads and may collect:
    • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
    • IP address
    • Interaction data with ads

2.2 Types of Data Collected

We do not collect personal information like names or emails. The data collected includes:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Gameplay analytics (e.g., completed levels)
  • Advertising ID (for ad personalization)

3. How We Use Data

Data is used to:

  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Show relevant ads via Unity Ads
  • Analyze player behavior through Unity Analytics

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Unity Technologies: Their privacy policies apply:
    • Unity Privacy Policy
    • Unity Ads GDPR Info

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Reset your Advertising ID (in device settings)
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Unity's opt-out page
